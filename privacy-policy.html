Privacy Policy

NoteWise — AI Powered Notes

Your privacy matters. NoteWise is designed to keep your notes private and your data safe. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

1 Information We Collect

NoteWise collects minimal information to provide its services:

  • Note content: Text you write in the app is stored locally on your device only.
  • AI requests: When you use AI features (summarize, improve, continue writing), your note text is sent to an AI API provider to process your request. This data is not stored by us.
  • No account required: We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers.

2 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app's core note-taking features.
  • To process AI-powered features such as summarization and writing assistance.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3 Data Storage

All notes are stored locally on your device using secure local storage. We do not maintain any servers that store your notes. Your data stays on your phone.

4 AI Features & Third-Party Services

When you use AI features, your note text is sent to a third-party AI provider (such as Anthropic or Google) to generate a response. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

We only send the minimum text needed to fulfill your request. We do not store AI responses on any server.

5 Children's Privacy

NoteWise is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

6 Data Security

We take data security seriously. Since all your notes are stored locally on your device, your data is protected by your device's built-in security. AI requests are sent over encrypted HTTPS connections.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
